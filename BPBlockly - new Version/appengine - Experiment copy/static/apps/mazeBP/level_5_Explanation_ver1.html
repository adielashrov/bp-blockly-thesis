In this level we have seen two scenarios.

The first scenario is responsible for handling a left turn.
The scenario waits for the event, 'path left' and then requests to 'turn left' and 'move forward'.

The second scenario is responsible for moving the pegman forward.

The 'turn left' event has a higher priority than 'move forward'. Therefore when both events are requested the turn event is triggered.
